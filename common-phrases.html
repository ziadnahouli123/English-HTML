Common English Phrases

Essential expressions for everyday conversations

Greetings and Introductions

Common ways to greet people and introduce yourself:

  • Hi, how are you?
  • Nice to meet you
  • Good morning/afternoon/evening
  • It's a pleasure to meet you

Usage Examples:

  • "Hi, how are you?" - "I'm fine, thanks. How about you?"
  • "Nice to meet you, I'm John." - "Nice to meet you too, I'm Sarah."

Making Small Talk

Useful phrases for casual conversations:

  • What do you do for a living?
  • How's your day going?
  • Beautiful weather today, isn't it?
  • Have you been here before?

Usage Examples:

  • "What do you do for a living?" - "I'm a teacher."
  • "How's your day going?" - "Pretty good, thanks for asking!"

Asking for Help

Common phrases when you need assistance:

  • Excuse me, could you help me?
  • I'm looking for...
  • Could you tell me where...?
  • Would you mind...?

Usage Examples:

  • "Excuse me, could you help me find the library?"
  • "Would you mind showing me how this works?"
