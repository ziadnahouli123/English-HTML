Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start learning?

Simply create an account and start with our beginner-friendly lessons. Our system will guide you through the learning process based on your current level.

Is FluentPath free?

We offer both free and premium content. Basic lessons and features are available for free, while advanced features and specialized content require a premium subscription.

How does the AI chat practice work?

Our AI chat feature uses advanced language models to simulate real conversations, helping you practice speaking and writing in a natural context.

Can I track my progress?

Yes! Your dashboard shows detailed progress tracking, including completed lessons, quiz scores, and areas for improvement.
