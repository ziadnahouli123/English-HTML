Vowel Sounds

Master the essential vowel sounds in English pronunciation

Short Vowels

Short vowels are quick, sharp sounds found in many common English words.

Common Short Vowels:

  • /æ/ as in "cat"
  • /ɛ/ as in "bed"
  • /ɪ/ as in "sit"
  • /ɒ/ as in "hot"
  • /ʌ/ as in "cup"

Practice Words:

  • hat, map, back
  • red, head, said
  • pin, win, fish
  • pot, sock, lock
  • sun, fun, luck

Long Vowels

Long vowels are held longer and often correspond to the letter names in the alphabet.

Common Long Vowels:

  • /eɪ/ as in "make"
  • /iː/ as in "see"
  • /aɪ/ as in "like"
  • /oʊ/ as in "go"
  • /uː/ as in "blue"

Practice Words:

  • cake, rain, day
  • meet, feel, team
  • time, night, fly
  • boat, snow, grow
  • moon, food, true

Vowel Combinations

Vowel combinations or diphthongs are two vowel sounds joined in one syllable.

Common Combinations:

  • /aʊ/ as in "house"
  • /ɔɪ/ as in "boy"
  • /ɪə/ as in "hear"
  • /eə/ as in "care"
  • /ʊə/ as in "pure"

Practice Words:

  • down, mouth, now
  • toy, voice, enjoy
  • dear, near, clear
  • fair, share, pair
  • sure, tour, cure
