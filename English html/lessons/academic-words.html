Academic Words

Essential vocabulary for academic writing and research

Research and Analysis

Common words used in academic research:

  • Analyze - To examine methodically
  • Evaluate - To assess or determine value
  • Investigate - To carry out systematic inquiry
  • Synthesize - To combine elements into a whole

Usage Examples:

  • "This paper analyzes the effects of climate change."
  • "The study evaluates different teaching methods."

Academic Writing Style

Formal vocabulary for academic writing:

  • Furthermore - To add information
  • Nevertheless - Despite that
  • Consequently - As a result
  • Subsequently - Following that

Usage Examples:

  • "Furthermore, the research indicates..."
  • "Nevertheless, certain limitations exist..."

Critical Thinking Terms

Words for expressing critical analysis:

  • Argue - To present reasons for or against
  • Critique - To evaluate critically
  • Justify - To show to be right
  • Validate - To confirm or substantiate

Usage Examples:

  • "The author argues that economic factors..."
  • "This evidence helps to validate the hypothesis."
