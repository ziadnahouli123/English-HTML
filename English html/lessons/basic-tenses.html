Basic English Tenses

Master the fundamental tenses in English grammar

Present Simple Tense

The present simple tense is used to:

  • Talk about regular actions or habits
  • Express general truths
  • Describe permanent situations

Examples:

  • I work in London.
  • The sun rises in the east.
  • She lives in Paris.

Present Continuous Tense

The present continuous tense is used to:

  • Talk about actions happening now
  • Describe temporary situations
  • Talk about planned future arrangements

Examples:

  • I am working right now.
  • They are studying for their exam.
  • We are moving to London next month.

Past Simple Tense

The past simple tense is used to:

  • Talk about completed actions in the past
  • Describe past habits or states
  • Tell stories or narrate events

Examples:

  • I worked in London last year.
  • She lived in Paris when she was young.
  • They visited Rome in 2020.
