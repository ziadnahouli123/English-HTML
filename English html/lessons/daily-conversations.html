Daily Conversations

Master everyday English conversations with confidence

Greetings and Introductions

Learn common ways to start conversations and introduce yourself.

Formal Greetings:

  • "Good morning/afternoon/evening"
  • "Hello, how are you?"
  • "It's nice to meet you"

Informal Greetings:

  • "Hi there!"
  • "Hey! How's it going?"
  • "What's up?"

Example Dialogue:

A: Hi there! I'm Sarah.
B: Hey Sarah! I'm Mike. Nice to meet you.
A: Nice to meet you too! How's your day going?
B: Pretty good, thanks! How about yours?

Making Small Talk

Small talk helps build relationships and keep conversations flowing.

Common Topics:

  • Weather: "Beautiful day, isn't it?"
  • Work: "How's work going?"
  • Weekend plans: "Any plans for the weekend?"
  • Recent events: "Did you see the game last night?"

Example Dialogue:

A: Lovely weather today, isn't it?
B: Yes, it's perfect for a walk in the park.
A: Definitely! Do you come here often?
B: Yes, I try to walk here during my lunch break. It's so peaceful.

Asking for Help

Learn polite ways to ask for assistance in different situations.

Useful Phrases:

  • "Excuse me, could you help me with...?"
  • "I'm sorry to bother you, but..."
  • "Would you mind showing me...?"
  • "Could you tell me where...?"

Example Dialogue:

A: Excuse me, could you help me find the library?
B: Of course! It's just around the corner.
A: Thank you so much. Is it open now?
B: Yes, it's open until 8 PM today.
