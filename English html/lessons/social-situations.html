Social Situations

Handle common social interactions with confidence

At a Party

Learn how to navigate social gatherings and make connections.

Useful Phrases:

  • "Thanks for inviting me!"
  • "What brings you here?"
  • "How do you know [host's name]?"
  • "Would you like something to drink?"

Example Dialogue:

Host: Welcome! I'm so glad you could make it!
Guest: Thanks for inviting me! Your place looks amazing.
Host: Thank you! Have you met Sarah? She's a colleague from work.
Guest: No, I haven't. Hi Sarah, nice to meet you!

At a Restaurant

Master the language needed for dining out.

Key Phrases:

  • "Could I see the menu, please?"
  • "I'd like to order..."
  • "How spicy/sweet is this dish?"
  • "Could we get the check, please?"

Example Dialogue:

Server: Welcome! Would you like to start with any drinks?
Customer: Yes, I'll have a glass of water, please.
Server: And are you ready to order?
Customer: Could you tell me about today's specials first?

Making Plans

Learn how to arrange social activities and respond to invitations.

Common Expressions:

  • "Are you free this weekend?"
  • "Would you like to join us for...?"
  • "That sounds great!"
  • "I'll have to check my schedule"

Example Dialogue:

A: Hey, we're planning to go bowling on Saturday. Want to join?
B: That sounds fun! What time were you thinking?
A: Around 7 PM. We could grab dinner after.
B: Perfect! Count me in.
