Meeting Etiquette

Learn professional language for business meetings.

Opening a Meeting:

  • "Let's get started with today's agenda"
  • "Thank you all for joining us today"
  • "I'd like to welcome everyone to this meeting"
  • "Let's begin by going over the main points"

Example Dialogue:

Chair: Good morning everyone. Thank you for joining today's meeting.
Participant: Good morning. Before we start, could I add an item to the agenda?
Chair: Of course. We'll address that after the main points.
Participant: Thank you, I appreciate it.

Email Communication

Master professional email writing and responses.

Key Phrases:

  • "I hope this email finds you well"
  • "I'm writing to inquire about..."
  • "Please find attached..."
  • "I look forward to hearing from you"

Example Email:

Dear Mr. Johnson,

I hope this email finds you well. I'm writing to follow up on our meeting from last week regarding the project timeline.

Please find attached the updated schedule for your review.

I look forward to your feedback.

Best regards,
Sarah

Negotiations

Learn how to negotiate effectively in English.

Useful Expressions:

  • "What are your thoughts on...?"
  • "I see your point, however..."
  • "Could we meet halfway?"
  • "Let's explore other options"

Example Dialogue:

A: We're looking at a budget of $50,000 for this project.
B: I understand your position, however, given the scope, we'd need at least $65,000.
A: What if we adjusted the timeline to reduce costs?
B: That's an interesting suggestion. Let's explore that option.
