Business English

Essential vocabulary for professional settings

Meeting Vocabulary

Common phrases used in business meetings:

  • Let's get started
  • Moving on to the next point
  • To summarize
  • Any questions?

Usage Examples:

  • "Let's get started with today's agenda."
  • "Moving on to the next point, our Q4 projections..."

Email Communication

Professional email phrases:

  • I hope this email finds you well
  • As per our discussion
  • Please find attached
  • I look forward to hearing from you

Usage Examples:

  • "I hope this email finds you well. I'm writing regarding..."
  • "Please find attached the report we discussed."

Negotiation Terms

Key phrases for business negotiations:

  • Let's discuss the terms
  • What's your best offer?
  • We can work with that
  • That's a deal-breaker

Usage Examples:

  • "Let's discuss the terms of the contract."
  • "We can work with that budget, but we'll need to adjust the scope."
